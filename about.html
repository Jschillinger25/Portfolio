About me

I’m originally from Wisconsin but the mountains were calling me and I could not ignore! Now I’m living in Denver. Im an outdoor enthusiast who loves to travel and explore new places! I cannot consider a new place “conquered” until I’ve tried the local flavor-particularly pizza and ice cream!

People are more important to me than anything and they are the reason I design. I design to make users lives better and to add value to their days. I studied art for 6 weeks in Italy during college and that began my passion for making things beautiful. I also care about functionality too which is why I like simple designs and minimalism has greatly impacted the way I view space.
