Let Me Introduce Myself!

My passion is people. They are the reason I chose design. I love discovering how they think and feel and why. I design with the user in mind because without them I don't see the point!

My philosophy about design is simple: functionality over form. I like clean designs that contain a lot of whitespace and that draw the user's eye to what matters.

I also believe that design must frequently re-define itself because of how quickly society changes. As I result, I, as a designer, don't place as much value in what I know now as much as I do my ability to research and learn new things.

When I’m not researching, socializing, or designing, you can find me hiking in the beautiful Colorado mountains with my husband. Or you may find me trying out a new recipe since I’m a lowkey foodie. Or I may be researching the next location where I want to travel. No matter where you find me though, I'll be excited to meet you!
