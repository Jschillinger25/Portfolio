Hello, Nice to Meet You!

My number one passion is people. People are the reason I chose to be designer. But why? I could have become a doctor, a lawyer, a missionary, a customer service representative at a company (and in fact I was) so why design? It’s simple. I love it. I love talking with people and discovering how they think and feel and why, then then taking that information and creating something functional yet beautiful with it. I design with the user in mind because without the user what would be the point?

People are fascinating and they’re worth getting to know. I love that being a UX/UI designer gives me a daily glipse into how people think, what they care about, and why.

When I’m not researching, meeting with people, or designing user-friendly interfaces, you can find me hiking in the beautiful Colorado mountains with my husband of 4 years. Or you may find me trying out the local flavor since I’m a lowkey foodie. Or I may be planning out my next trip since traveling is a big passion of mine too. No matter where you find me though, you’ll likely find me happy because life is beautiful and I have much to be thankful for.
