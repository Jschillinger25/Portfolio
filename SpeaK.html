SpeaK

2017 - UI Designer

Introduction

SpeaK was an opportunity for me to grow my skills designing iOS apps. I was given the guidelines and branding for the design and now it was up to me to design an app that would be user friendly and visually appealing.

I received the SWOT analyses from the research team on competitors like Google, Textra, and Facebook Messenger. These SWOTS helped me to understand the competition and design SpeaK in a way that would be friendly and appealing.

The Sketches

After going through the research materials I started sketching out ideas to determine the layout and the general feeling I wanted the app to have. I sent my sketches to the research team to test usability and friendliness.

High Fidelity Mockups

Once the sketches were all approved I was able to move forward to design the high-fidelity mockups.
