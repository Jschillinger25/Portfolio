Stockup

2017 - UI Designer

Introduction

Stockup was an opportunity for me to explore iOS guidelines as well as learn more about the finacnial industry. I was given the guidelines and branding for the design and now it was up to me to design an app that would be cutting edge.

The Sketches

I received the SWOT analyses from the research team on competitors like robinhood, E*Trade, and Acorns. These SWOTS helped me to understand the competition and design Stockup in a way that would be cutting edge.

Originally I did not have a chart on the homescreen but users informed the research team that more visuals would help the information to be more easily digested. Finances can be difficult to understand and follow so the premis was to create an app that would invite users in and provide them with a satisfying experience.

I started sketching out various chart ideas to showcase the account balance.

Final Thoughts

Overall, even though this was a smaller project, it was still a lot of fun to design an app for investing. For some people investing is super exciting and for others it’s nerve wracking. Some avoid it all together. I hope through my design to make users feel comfortable and willing to explore an industry that they might have avoided in the past.
