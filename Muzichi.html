Muzichi

2017 - UI Designer

Introduction

Muzichi was an opportunity for me to explore iOS guidelines as well as learn more about the music streaming industry. I was given the guidelines and branding for the design and now it was up to me to design an app that would be cutting edge.

The Sketches

I received the SWOT analyses from the research team on competitors like Spotify, Pandora, and iHeart Radio. These SWOTS helped me understand the competition I was up against and also determine the layout for Muzichi so it would be cutting edge.

Because of the research I knew I would need to include features such as a search, playlist, and album and song views.

After going through the research materials I started sketching out ideas to determine layout and the general feeling I wanted the app to have. I sent my sketches to the research team to test usability and friendliness.

I knew I needed to come up with an innovative idea for the song play screen. I wanted it to be engaging. I started sketching out some ideas of what the screen could look like. Out of my different sketches I chose to go with the one that had interactive bars as the bottom that would move with the music.
